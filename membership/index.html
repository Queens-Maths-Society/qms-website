How Do I Join?

Membership to the society is free, to join either: subscribe to the mailing list on the cambridge lists system, or email the secretary.

Mailing List Email Secretary

What Do I Get?

Joining the society means... not much. We do not check membership so anyone can attend our talks and social events, although being on the mailing list definitely helps know when and where these are taking place. All members can also attend some of our special events, however the Mahal and Maths Dinner are only attendable by maths students at Queens'.
