How to Join

Joining the Queens' Maths Society is simple, absolutely FREE, and all Queens' members are welcome. To join, simply come along to any event that takes your fancy, or send us an e-mail so that we can add you to our mailing list. That way you can keep up-to-date with the latest QMS news. You can also join our Facebook group where we will post about upcoming events.
