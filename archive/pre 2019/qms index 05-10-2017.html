Welcome

Welcome to the Queens' College Mathematics Society website. QMS, founded in 2014, has one mission: To create a fun, inclusive and sociable environment for members of Queens' College, both mathematicians and otherwise, to learn about and celebrate mathematics and its various applications.

What we do

Queens' Mathematics Society serves a number of functions including:

Research Talks

Listen to professors, lecturers and supervisors describe their cutting edge research and mathematical interests in an accessible format

Special Events

Including our legendary summer garden party, where the CMS lawn flows with food and drink

Sponsored Talks

Find out about some of the exciting career opportunities available for quantitative graduates from industry professionals

How to Join

Joining the Queens' Maths Society is simple, absolutely FREE, and all Queens' members are welcome. To join, simply come along to any event that takes your fancy, or send us an e-mail so that we can add you to our mailing list. That way you can keep up-to-date with the latest QMS news. You can also join our Facebook group where we will post about upcoming events.
